Backgammon Scoreboard

A clean, minimal backgammon match scoreboard for iOS.

Features

  • Tap to score — left side or right side
  • Match lengths from 3 to 21 points
  • Automatic Crawford and post-Crawford detection
  • Score correction via long press
  • Screen stays awake during play
  • Dark and light themes (via iOS Settings)
  • No ads, no tracking, no account required
